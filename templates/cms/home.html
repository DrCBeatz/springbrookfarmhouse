Welcome to Spring Brooke Farmhouse

Wake up to birdsong and sweeping farm views just fifteen minutes from historic Perth, Ontario. Spring Brooke Farmhouse sits on 100 private acres of pasture and forest, home to sheep, cows, llamas, and their hardworking sheepdogs. Come to unplug, breathe country air, and watch the farm ebb and flow through the seasons.

Rustic exterior, contemporary comfort

  • Chef-ready kitchen – full-size appliances, farmhouse sink, complete cookware.
  • Coffee & wine bar – start mornings with fresh brew; toast sunsets with local vintages.
  • Living room – deep sofas and bar seating around a classic wood-burning stove.
  • Screened sun‑porch & wrap‑around deck – lounge, dine, or grill while overlooking the fields.

Sleep soundly

All beds are dressed in hotel‑quality linens.

Ideal for family gatherings, couples’ retreats, writing escapes, or a solo recharge.

Guest access

You have exclusive use of the entire farmhouse, front lawn, and backyard. (Basement is locked for maintenance.)

  • Vegetable garden – off‑limits, but in‑season produce is available for purchase from Isla, our garden curator.
  • Barns & pastures – working farm areas are closed for safety; ask to schedule a guided tour if you’d like to meet the animals up close.

Location

We’re in Lanark County—famed for maple syrup and rolling “Land of Lakes” scenery—18 km west of Perth, 1 hr from Ottawa, 1 hr 15 min from Kingston, 3 hrs from Montreal, and 3.5 hrs from Toronto.

Things to do nearby

  • Paddle, swim, or fish on pristine lakes
  • Hike or bike forested trails bursting with wildlife
  • Sample maple treats, farm‑gate produce, local craft beer & cider
  • Browse artisan studios, antique shops, and weekly farmers’ markets
  • Day‑spa soaks and Nordic‑style baths in nearby villages

Whether you crave adventure or quiet, Spring Brooke Farmhouse is your perfect Lanark County base.
